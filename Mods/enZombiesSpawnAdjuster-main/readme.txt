enZombie Spawn Adjust v1.0
https://community.7daystodie.com/topic/24594-enzombies-more-zombie-variations/

This mod allows you to increase the decrease the amount of zombies that spawn in certain areas of the game when using the enZombies mod.

If you have the Snufkin zombie Add-on or Robeloto zombie Add-ons installed, this sapwn adjuster mod will also control their spawn rates as well.
